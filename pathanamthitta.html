Pathanamthitta

Some of the tourist places are shown below

Elephant museum

Konni Aanakkoodu located in Konni is a well known training center for elephant's in Kerala. Its at a distance of 11 km from Pathanamthitta town.

Sabarimala

The Sabarimala Temple is a temple complex located at Sabarimala hill inside the Periyar Tiger Reserve in the Perinad Village

Maramon Convention

The Maramon Convention, one of the largest Christian convention in Asia, is held at Maramon, Pathanamthitta
