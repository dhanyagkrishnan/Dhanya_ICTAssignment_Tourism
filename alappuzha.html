Alappuzha

Some of the tourist places are shown below

Krishnapuram Palace

The Krishnapuram Palace is a palace and museum located in Kayamkulam near Alappuzha in Alappuzha district.

Arthunkal Church

St. Andrew's Church, Arthunkal is one of the famous pilgrim centres located at Alappuzha in South India

Nehru Trophy Boat Race

The Nehru Trophy Boat Tri Race/Champions Boat League is a popular Vallam Kali held in the Punnamada Lake near Alappuzha
